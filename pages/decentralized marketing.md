alias:: dema
- #dema is a concept of the search market without a single ranking center
- although we are building an ecosystem around the idea of a common knowledge graph it is only one ranking factor in #dema
- there could be a hundred other ranking factors for which a common knowledge graph can be a trust anchor
-
- Dema has several critical properties that are not available in traditional digital marketing.
- understanding of all backlinks by all participants
- ranking factors are defined locally by an agent, not by a search site
- on-chain conversion attribution of a search query into a payment transaction
- the ability to embed CTA directly into search results and pieces of content
- the presence of a publicly available oracle with a rank, based on which it is possible to make prediction markets on the rank, issue tokens modifying the base rank, attention tokens, etc.
  
  Dema is a new paradigm that transforms the old, gated, and clunky web into a better web, the Great Web.
